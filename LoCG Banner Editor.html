LoCG Header Banner Editor

Create a 1920 × 450 League of Comic Geeks-style header banner. The center guide shows the approximate 550px mobile-safe area. Images are processed locally in your browser.
Tip: click an added image to select it. Drag to move it, use the corner handles to resize, and use the rotation handle to rotate. Hold SHIFT to skew horizontal/vertical.

The guide lines are only for preview and will not appear in the exported image.

To adjust your own preferred background, just use the Add PNG / Image button and manipulate it like an object!
(I've kept the Upload Background feature because it tends to crop images into elegant banners automatically)
